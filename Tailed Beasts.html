Tailed Beasts and Jinchurikan



    The tailed beasts have been roaming the world of Naruto and have never left. We will start from first to last with the tailed beasts and jinchurikan. If you have no clue what a jinchurikan is, it is the Tailed beast possessor




    We are going in order, so in total there are 10 tailed beasts and jinchurikan.











    Shukaku is the first tailed beast due to his one tail. His Jinchuriki is Gaara the Kazekage of the hidden sand. He has Shukaku in him since he was born just like Naruto. He was aswell an orphan, no one liked him until he became a Kazekage.
